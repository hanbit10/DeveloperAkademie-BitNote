About Bitnote

Welcome to BitNote, a simple and efficient note-taking website.

BitNote was created by Hanbit Chang with the goal of providing a minimalist and distraction-free platform for taking notes.

The site utilizes Font Awesome for icons, enhancing the user experience with visually appealing elements.

BitNote stores all data locally in your browser's storage. Please be aware that if the data is deleted from your browser, it cannot be restored.

Key Features

  • Simple Interface: BitNote offers a clean and intuitive interface, making note-taking effortless.
  • Markdown Support: Write notes using Markdown syntax for formatting flexibility.
  • Customization: Personalize your experience by choosing from a variety of themes and font options.
  • Privacy: Your data remains private and is stored only in your browser's local storage.
  • Accessibility: Access your notes from any device with internet access, without the need to sign up or log in.
